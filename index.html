Visit Sambalpur

Discover temples, rivers and the mighty Hirakud Dam in this vibrant cultural heart of western Odisha.

Top three things to do in Sambalpur

Seek blessings at Maa Samaleswari Temple

Visit the revered shrine of the presiding goddess of Sambalpur, glowing with marigold garlands and the buzz of daily aarti.

Marvel at the mighty Hirakud Dam

Walk one of the world's longest earthen dams, soak in the sunset from Gandhi Minar, and watch the Mahanadi spread to the horizon.

Stroll through Gangadhar Meher University

Explore the leafy campus and striking gateway of the city's landmark university, named after the beloved Odia poet.
